About Me

Educational Experience

Hi, everyone! My name is Lyudmila. I moved to Israel from Russia a year ago. In Russia during my final years at school I was fortunate enough to take part in the English language competition after which I was enrolled in The Private Educational Center "POISK". Three years' study in this Center was one of the most rewarding experiences while at school. During my years in the Center I took an Intensive FCE test preparation course. After I graduated from the Center I continued to study English on my own. A couple of years later I decided to study Psychology and entered The Caucases Peoples' Friendship Institute. Through the learning of the basics of psychology I developed my soft skills that I had found very useful in my career of an English language tutor. After 6 years of studying I graduated from The institute with honors. After making aliya I started a half-year Hebrew language course in the Ulpan and succesfully finished it in February this year. Now I am here - in Israel and I am learning to code in ITC's Fullstack Development program.

Career Experience

When I was 17 and I was graduated both from high school and from the Center "POISK" I realized that I am extremly fond of English and soon I started my career as an English language tutor. In the teaching I used differnt methods like Structural approach, Differentiated Instruction and Task Based Learning. As a tutor I provided an individual, tailored educational programs for each of my pupils and moreover, I was changing my style of teaching to suit their different learning styles. My teaching background is more than 15 years.
After moving to Israel I worked as a club coordinator in Amigour company. I was responsibe for organizating holiday celebrations like Purim, Pesach and ceremonies on Holocost Rememberance Day, Victory Day and other events. But I quit my job because I am highly motivated for a career change goal.

That's why I desided to launch my career in the High-Tech field.
